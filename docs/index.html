Committed to the Wrentham Community

Leo is an experienced public servant, researcher, and long-time Wrentham resident. Join him.

    Meet Leo

    During his 35 years in Wrentham, Leo has served on numerous committees.

    He is currently completing 18 years on the Conservation Commission. He has served on the Recycling Committee, the Open Space Committee, and the Zoning Board of Appeals. He was the driving force behind Wrentham's adoption of the Community Preservation Act.

    With those years of service to our town, Leo is now eager to serve on our Select Board.
